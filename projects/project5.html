Introduction

This game is based on a player to dig down in the soil and try finding ores and treasures to then sell it and upgrade your gear to get even deeper after. We didn't need to actually make a game, but more think of an idea and make a GDD on it, but I was so inspired by this idea, so I will be finishing this game.
