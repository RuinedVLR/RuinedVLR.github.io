Survive-A-Ball

Arcade / Survivors-like — mini-game, made in Unity

About

It's a Survivors-like game that I made for my Game Developement course, main goal is to survive as long as you can while killing enemies, leveling up, and upgrading your stats.

Project Info

  • Role: Gameplay Programmer
  • Engine: Unity
  • Year: 2025
  • Team: Solo

Introduction

I started with a regular Roll-A-Ball tutorial from Unity, and my idea was to make my own Survivors-like game, because I enjoyed playing them a lot. It turned out better than I expected it to be, but I still didn't know what theme to apply to it, and my friend, who also made all the music for it, gave me his product full of space related beats, and I thought why won't I do it in space theme.
