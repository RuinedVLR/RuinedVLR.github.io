Grave Grab

Game jam project — Quick Prototype

About

Grave Grab is a fast-paced action simulation game where you play as a mischievous grave robber trying to loot precious treasures… without getting caught by the undead night shift workers

Project Info

  • Role: Gameplay
  • Engine: Unity
  • Duration: 48 hours
  • Team: Roman Z. Kai S. Samreen K. Katelyn N. Matthew M.

Postmortem

We learned a lot from that game jam, since it was our first Game Jam, everybody was stressing and not sure how to properly do things, but we definetely made a good team bounds, learned more about programming/art/animations and overall how games are developed.
