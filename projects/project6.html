Introduction

You survive a workplace party by rotating social masks, choosing how to respond without dialogue options, and living with the permanent consequences of deciding to 'mask' or 'unmask' during these conversations You navigate the party, where attention is finite, noise stacks, and every response costs something. Interact with your co-workers, where there always seems like there are no “right” options of things to say; only ever choices leading to results out of your control.
